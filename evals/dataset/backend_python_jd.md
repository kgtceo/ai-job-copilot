**The role**
6-month contract building and scaling backend services for a fintech platform.
You'll own services end to end and work in a regulated environment.

- Expert Python, Django or FastAPI, PostgreSQL.

- Async task processing (Celery or similar) and Redis.
- CI/CD and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform a plus).
- Experience shipping in a regulated / payments domain.

**Nice to have**
- Event-driven architecture and analytics pipelines.
- Team/delivery leadership.

**Keywords:** python, django, fastapi, postgresql, aws, celery, redis, terraform,
ci/cd, payments, fintech
